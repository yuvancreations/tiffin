Our Story

Founded in 2020, FreshBites started with a simple mission: to bring the comfort of home-cooked meals to busy professionals and families.

What began as a small kitchen serving 20 customers has grown into a trusted tiffin service delivering over 500 meals daily. Our passion for fresh, nutritious food and our commitment to quality has made us the preferred choice for health-conscious individuals.

Every meal we prepare is crafted with love, using locally-sourced ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations. We believe that good food is the foundation of good health, and we're committed to making it accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

To provide nutritious, delicious, and affordable home-cooked meals that nourish the body and soul. We strive to make healthy eating convenient for everyone, one meal at a time.
